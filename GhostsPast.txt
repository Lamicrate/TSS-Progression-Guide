You must grab this task before starting the Ashengate West raid if you plan on killing these bosses/going to AGN.<br>
<br>
<c "#00FF00">Mission Type:</c> Solo<br>
<c "#00FF00">NPC:</c> Jenray, Envoy of Ro<br>
<c "#00FF00">Location:</c> Direwind Cliffs, near Ashengate zone in a tent (3040, -316).<br>
<c "#00FF00">Request Phrase:</c> "superiority" (must unequip weapons)<br>
<c "#00FF00">Request Requirements:</c> Ally with Scholars of Solusek<br>
<c "#00FF00">Rewards:</c> +Faction, Progression<br>
<br>
<c "#00FF00">Kill Sothgar the Twiceborn</c><br>
This is the boss at the end of the Ashengate West raid instance.<br>
<br>
<c "#00FF00">Return to Jenray, Envoy of Ro</c><br>
Completing this task grants you part of the progression required to request the Ashengate North raid.<br>
