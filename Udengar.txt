<c "#0080ff">Udengar Raid</c><br>
This is a 54-man raid task consisting of 2 loot dropping boss. There are no requirements to participate.<br>
<br>
<c "#00FF00">NPC:</c> Fergarin Bardon I<br>
<c "#00FF00">Location:</c> Valdeholm, in the back room beihind Wraithguard Heimgul, (770, -780)<br>
<c "#00FF00">Request Phrase:</c> "I believe in coincidence"<br>
<c "#00FF00">Entry Location:</c> Valdeholm, click door at compass location.<br>
<c "#00FF00">Request Requirements:</c> Fergarin's 3 group tasks and 1 previous raid, Kangur. Ally faction.<br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">----Letters of Evidence----</c><br>
Letters of Evidence are recieved from completion of the Kangur raid. They are used to "charm" trash giants and use them during the raid fight as fodder.<br>
You must say to them: "I have evidence of treason" which will briefly make them non-KOS. You then hand them the letter of evidence and they may or may not become your pet.<br>
They have 3 possibilities, Attack you, go Neutral and wander off, or Join your cause.<br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">----High Lorekeeper Udengar Dergon----</c><br>
<c "#0080ff">On the Clock</c><br>
Once anyone enters the instance, you are on the clock. You have 17 minutes to make it north to the pit/amphitheater.<br>
At 17 min, the giants will vacate the pit and at the 20 min mark Udengar and 2 "a Zek loyalist" will emerge.<br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">Engage</c><br>
You must prevent Udengar from reaching the gates or the task ends in failure.<br>
The giants who have joined will assist as soon as you engage. They are used to offtank while the 2 adds are taken down.<br>
<br><c "#ff0000">a Zek loyalist *DO NOT SLOW*</c><br>
2 of them on engage. They hit hard, proc a stun, and AE rampage. They are also immune to spells cast outside of melee range. DO NOT SLOW THEM, is has an opposite effect.<br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">Boss Fight</c><br>
<c "#ff0000">High Lorekeeper Udengar Dergon *DO NOT SLOW*</c><br>
Hits hard, AE Rampage, immune to spells cast outside of melee range. DO NOT SLOW HIM, is has an opposite effect.<br>
Casts "Apathy of Zek" - PBAE 40% skill reducer, 8 Corruption Counters.<br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">85% HP</c><br>
At 85% HP Udengar will begin frequently summoning adds:<br>
"hammer of Zek" - hits for 900 and can be mezzed.<br>
"unswerving hammer of Zek" - hits for 1300, cannot be mezzed.<br>
<c "#0080ff">50% HP</c><br>
At 50% HP, one more Loyalist will spawn and attack the raid.<br>
Nothing despawns when Udengar is killed.<br>
<br>
<c "#00FF00">Speak with Fengarin Bardon I</c><br>
Have someone hail Fergarin to complete the raid task and reward everyone with +300 Wraithguard Leadership faction.<br>
Completing this task opens up the 3rd raid in his Arc, <a href="file:///storyline/TSS_Progression_Guide/Odeen.txt">Speak with the King</a>.<br>
